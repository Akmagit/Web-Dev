Caesar Salad

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Romaine lettuce 1 head
Croutons 1 cup
Grated Parmesan cheese 1/2 cup
Caesar dressing 1/2 cup
Grilled chicken breast 2, sliced

Steps:

  1. Wash and chop the Romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Grill the chicken breast until cooked through, then slice.
  3. In a large bowl, toss together the Romaine lettuce, croutons, Parmesan cheese, and Caesar dressing.
  4. Top the salad with the sliced grilled chicken breast.
  5. Serve and enjoy!

Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Ground beef 500g
Onion, chopped 1
Garlic cloves, minced 2
Canned tomatoes 400g
Tomato paste 2 tbsp
Red wine 100ml
Olive oil 2 tbsp
Salt and pepper To taste

Steps:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, cook until softened.
  3. Add ground beef, cook until browned.
  4. Stir in canned tomatoes, tomato paste, and red wine. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve Bolognese sauce over cooked spaghetti.

Blini

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 1 cup
Milk 1 1/2 cups
Eggs 2
Salt 1/2 tsp
Sugar 1 tbsp
Butter, melted 2 tbsp

Steps:

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together flour, salt, and sugar.
  2. In another bowl, beat eggs, then stir in milk and melted butter.
  3. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients, stirring until smooth.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan for each blin, tilting to spread evenly.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface and the edges are golden brown, then flip and cook the other side.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite toppings such as jam, sour cream, or honey.
